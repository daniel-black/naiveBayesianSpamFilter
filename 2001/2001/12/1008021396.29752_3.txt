                  </span><b><span style="font-family: Verdana">Elite 
                  Technologies Signs LOI With Source Code Corporation</span></b></p>
                  <p><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">
                  ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Oct. 24, 2001--Elite Technologies, 
                  Inc. (OTC BB: ETCH) announced that it has signed a Letter of 
                  Intent to acquire Source Code Corporation and Integrated IT 
                  Solutions, Inc., a privately-held Boston-based company which 
                  owns nine subsidiary companies. </span></p>
                  <p><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">
                  Source Code showed revenue of approximately $48 million 
                  dollars in the last fiscal year, with an EBITDA of more than 
                  $1.5 million dollars. The acquisition of the company is 
                  pending satisfactory due diligence by both parties. </span>
                  </p>
                  <p><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">Scott 
                  Schuster, Chairman of Elite said, “Source Code is a 
                  multi-tiered, privately held company, whose subsidiaries 
                  include manufacturing and distribution of computer related 
                  items. We believe Source Code will make a welcome addition to 
                  the Elite family of companies, not only because of their 
                  strength in the various areas of expertise, but because of the 
                  synergy they provide to the existing subsidiary companies of 
                  Elite.'' </span></p>
                  <p><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">Elite 
                  Technologies, Inc., through its subsidiary companies which 
                  include AMG, IET and ICON, provides hardware wholesale and 
                  retail distribution of computer parts and systems. Elite also 
                  designs, manufactures and distributes Internet “pay by minute” 
                  Kiosks throughout the </span>
                  <span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">US</span><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana"> 
                  and foreign countries. Elite also owns Worldtouch, a 
                  telecommunication service provider that specializes in Voice 
                  Over IP technology. </span></p>

                  <h2><span style="font-size: 12.0pt; font-family: Verdana">
                  Elite Technologies Announces LOI With CenDyne</span></h2>
                  <p><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">
                  ATLANTA--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Oct. 18, 2001--Elite Technologies, 
                  Inc. (OTCBB:
                  <a href="http://finance.yahoo.com/q?s=etch.ob&d=t" style="color: #660000; text-decoration: underline; text-underline: single">
                  ETCH</a> -
                  <a href="http://biz.yahoo.com/n/e/etch.ob.html" style="color: #660000; text-decoration: underline; text-underline: single">
                  news</a>) announced that it has signed a Letter of Intent to 
                  acquire CenDyne, Inc., a privately held California-based 
                  computer peripheral distributor. </span></p>
                  <p><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">
                  According to CenDyne's website, since selling its first 
                  products in June 1999, CenDyne's sales have skyrocketed to 
                  $29.9 million in 2000. CenDyne's revenues are expected to 
                  reach approximately $35 million for the current fiscal year. 
                  The acquisition is pending the completion of due diligence by 
                  both companies. </span></p>
                  <p><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">
                  CenDyne was honored by Entrepreneur Magazine in June, 2001 
                  being named number four on the companies' 2001 list of 
                  “America's Hot 100 Fastest-Growing New Businesses.'' </span>
                  </p>
                  <p><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">Scott 
                  Schuster, Chairman of Elite said, “CenDyne has some very 
                  exciting opportunities before them. We are looking forward to 
                  a mutually beneficial relationship between Elite and CenDyne. 
                  This will allow Elite to begin expansion to the West Coast 
                  areas of the </span>
                  <span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">US</span><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">, 
                  and further open new markets for its other products.'' </span>
                  </p>
                  <p><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">Elite 
                  Technologies, Inc., through its subsidiary companies which 
                  include AMG, IET and ICON, provides hardware wholesale and 
                  retail distribution of computer parts and systems. Elite also 
                  designs, manufactures and distributes Internet “pay by minute” 
                  Kiosks throughout the </span>
                  <span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana">US</span><span style="font-size: 10.0pt; font-family: Verdana"> 
                  and foreign countries. Elite also owns Worldtouch, a 
                  telecommunication service provider which specializes in Voice 
                  Over IP technology. CenDyne's website is
                  <a href="http://www.cendyne.com" style="color: #660000; text-decoration: underline; text-underline: single">
