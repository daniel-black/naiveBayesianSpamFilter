<p class="MsoBodyText"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span><b>Los Angeles – Philosophic dog organizations join to provide disaster
relief to dog owning victims of the Sept. 11 terrorist attacks and to those who
are quickly called to Military service in response.</b><o:p>
</o:p>
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">The Bull Terrier Welfare Foundation, a California nonprofit
corporation recognized by the IRS as a charity, and A Dog Owner’s Network, a
six year old Internet Portal for dogs, have resolved to expand a Disaster Relief
services to assist those effected by the September 11 terrorists attacks in the
US and those who are quickly called to service in response to the National
disaster.<o:p>
</o:p>
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">The Bull Terrier Welfare Foundation provides a list of
volunteers offering temporary housing for Bull Terriers in the event of a
disaster - fire, flood, hurricane, tornado, storm or other situations where the
Bull Terrier owner must quickly relocate their dog(s) until permanent
arrangements can be made on the Internet at <a href="http://www.btwf.org/disast-2.htm">http://www.btwf.org/disast-2.htm</a>.
The Foundation, and it’s website btwf.org, is restricted to Bull Terriers.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;</span><o:p>
</o:p>
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Recognizing the immediate need by an estimated 20,000 US
families with dogs of all breeds, the Foundation has coordinated with A Dog
Owner’s Network, <a href="http://www.adognet.com/">http://www.adognet.com/</a>,
to expand the service to all breeds hosted at <a href="http://www.adognet.com/911/">http://www.adognet.com/911/</a>.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
</span>This site will go live on September 29, 2001.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;</span><o:p>
</o:p>
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">This new site will provide a clearinghouse for those who
are willing to provide temporary housing until permanent arrangements can be
made.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;</span><o:p>
</o:p>
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Cris Alarcon stated, “In this time of tragedy, we feel
that we can help the by assisting with housing of pets during times of disaster
or other National emergencies.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>We
will make America bigger, better, and stronger.”<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;
</span>Immediate sponsors include, Crossroads Homes of Placerville, CA and
Desert Fabrics of Quartzsite, AZ who have donated money and merchandise
respectively.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </span>“Desire to help in
any way is tremendous, it makes me proud to be an American,” said Cris.</p>
